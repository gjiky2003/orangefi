## 🍊 OrangeFi: AI-Powered Debt Consolidation

### Mission
Make debt consolidation fairer, faster, and more accessible through AI-powered underwriting and bank partnerships.

### Vision
The leading AI-native lending platform powering bank-partnered consumer loans.

## The Solution

**OrangeFi** is a technology platform that connects borrowers who need debt consolidation with banks that want consumer loan origination.

```
Borrower → OrangeFi (AI underwriting + servicing) → Partner Bank (origination + balance sheet)
```

### Key Differentiators

| Differentiator | Why It Matters |
|----------------|----------------|
| AI-native underwriting | Better risk selection = better pricing for borrowers, lower losses for bank |
| Cash flow analysis | Beats FICO-only models (especially for thin-file borrowers) |
| Bank partnership model | No regulatory licensing burden, no balance sheet risk |
| Founder's model risk expertise | Banks trust someone who speaks their regulatory language |
| Fully automated | One founder can operate the entire platform |

## Regulatory Strategy

- **Bank partnership model** → state lending licenses largely preempted
- **Founder's model risk background** → strong governance for bank partners
- **Budget $48k/year for legal counsel** (fintech regulatory specialist)
- **All compliance policies** drafted using AI + founder review
- **Pilot under 500 loans** → manageable regulatory exposure

---

## Risk Assessment

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| No bank partner found | Critical | Start with community banks, warm intros, 6-month search timeline |
| Model underperforms | High | Pilot validates before bank pitch; champion/challenger framework |
| Regulatory action | High | Finance-first design; regulatory counsel; founder compliance background |
| Fraud | Medium | Multi-layer detection; conservative pilot eligibility |
| Economic downturn | Medium | Bank holds credit risk; your fee income is portfolio-volumed-based |
